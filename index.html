About Us

We sell official new & used books

BookHub was founded to meet the high rise demand for both hardcopy and softcopy books. We strive to offer book enthusiasts the best possible buying experience - this includes an industry-leading customer service, mobile-friendly layout, free shipping (including international orders!), consistently affordable pricing, and access to the most sought-after books on the market. And each purchased book makes a difference in someone's life through our literacy partner programs.

Our Mission

To become the number one retail book store supplying Kenyan's with original content.
